About the Company

“Grand Fish Feed” is considered one of the most recent companies in the field of manufacturing and supplying of aquatic feed in Egypt. Grand aims at achieving leadership (Grand aims to be a pioneer) in the aquatic feed industry and contribute significantly to the development of the aquaculture sector locally, regionally and internationally.
